01 · Services

Built for reliability,
low latency & clean routing.

From a single 1 Gbps handoff to a full MPLS core with SD-WAN overlays, Aioni delivers the infrastructure that keeps enterprises, ISPs, and content networks running — quietly, predictably, and on schedule.

1 Gbps Enterprise Internet

Dedicated 1 Gbps enterprise internet with committed throughput, low jitter, and direct peering to the major content networks.

10 Gbps Service

10 Gbps pipes engineered for high-throughput and data-intensive workloads — video, cloud, backup, and hyperscale interconnect.

Co-location

Rack space in partner data centers with redundant power and cooling. Remote hands available through our 24/7 NOC.

VPN L2 / MPLS / SD-WAN

Private Layer 2 circuits, segment-routed MPLS cores, and policy-driven SD-WAN overlays. Built for multi-site enterprises.

IP Transit

Clean, optimized routes through multiple tier-1 upstreams and dozens of direct peerings. BGP-ready with full IPv4 and IPv6 support.

Managed Services

Proactive monitoring, configuration management, and full lifecycle operations. Real engineers answer your tickets, day or night.

Network Infrastructure Design

End-to-end network design, from branch and campus to the core — with security best practices baked in from day one. We handle the full journey: discovery, architecture, build, cutover, and steady-state operations.

03 · Why Aioni

No drama.
Just good routing.

Enterprise networking shouldn't feel like a leap of faith. Aioni wins engagements on fundamentals — design rigor, responsive engineering, and a network built so you don't have to think about it.

→ 01

Engineers on the other end of every ticket

You don't route through a script-reading first line. When you open a ticket, the person who answers can actually look at your BGP session, your interface counters, and your routing table.

→ 02

Presence in the exchanges that matter

Four IXP peerings out of VITRO Makati 2 — BBIX Manila, GetaFIX, AMS-IX Manila, and VIX — plus direct relationships with Meta, Google, Cloudflare, Alibaba Cloud, and TikTok.

→ 03

Design, build, and operate — by the same people

The engineer who architects your network doesn't disappear at handover. Aioni stays on as your operator, so institutional knowledge stays with your network.

→ 04

Open peering, transparent policy

AS153174 runs an open peering policy with no ratio requirements and no contract prerequisites. If you want to peer, we generally want to peer back.
